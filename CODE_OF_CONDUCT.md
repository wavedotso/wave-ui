# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Wave UI to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, or background.

## Our standards

Be respectful, assume good faith, and give constructive feedback. Not acceptable: harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory or derogatory comments, or publishing others' private information.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and to anyone representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately via a [GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/wavedotso/wave-ui/security/advisories/new) or by contacting the maintainers to `support@ui.wave.so`. Reports are handled confidentially, and maintainers may remove content or block contributors who violate this code.
